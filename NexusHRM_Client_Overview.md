## Introduction
Nexus HRM is a comprehensive Human Resource Management platform designed to streamline HR processes for organizations. It supports the full employee lifecycle, from onboarding to performance management, asset tracking, leave management, and more. The system is role-based, providing tailored experiences for Management (MD/Admin), Line Managers, and Employees.

---

## 1. User Roles & Access

### A. Managing Director (MD) / Admin
- Full access to all modules and data
- Can configure system settings, manage users, assets, cycles, and view all reports

### B. Line Managers
- Can review and approve appraisals for their team
- Approve/reject leave requests
- Assign and review KPIs
- View team performance and history

### C. Employees
- Can fill self-appraisals
- Request leave
- View their own profile, history, and assigned assets

---

## 2. Core Features

### A. Appraisal Cycle Management
- **Create Appraisal Cycles:** Admin creates cycles (e.g., Quarterly, Annual) with name, type, start/end dates
- **Activate Cycles:** Only active cycles can be used for appraisals
- **Initiate Appraisals:** Admin generates appraisal forms for all employees in an active cycle
- **Status Tracking:** Cycles and appraisals move through statuses (Draft, Active, Completed)

### B. Performance Appraisal
- **Employee Self-Review:** Employees rate themselves on competencies and provide comments
- **Manager Review:** Line managers review, score, and comment on employee appraisals
- **Finalization:** Appraisals are marked complete after manager review
- **Data Collected:** Ratings, comments, final scores, reviewer info, cycle info

### C. KPI Management
- **Assign KPIs:** Managers assign Key Performance Indicators to employees
- **Update Progress:** Employees update their progress on assigned KPIs
- **Review KPIs:** Managers review and approve KPI progress
- **Data Collected:** KPI targets, progress updates, review comments

### D. Leave Management
- **Request Leave:** Employees submit leave requests
- **Approval Workflow:** Managers/Admins approve or reject requests
- **Leave History:** All leave records are tracked
- **Data Collected:** Leave type, dates, status, approver info

### E. Asset Management
- **Asset Inventory:** Admins manage company assets (add, edit, remove)
- **Assign/Return Assets:** Assets can be assigned to or returned by employees
- **Asset Tracking:** Full history of asset assignments
- **Data Collected:** Asset details, assignment history, current holder

### F. Employee Management
- **Profile Management:** Add, edit, and view employee profiles
- **Employment History:** Track roles, promotions, and changes
- **Data Collected:** Personal info, job details, employment history

### G. Audit Logs
- **System Activity:** Track all key actions for transparency and compliance
- **Data Collected:** Action type, user, timestamp, affected records

### H. System Settings
- **Configuration:** Admins can set up system-wide settings, roles, and permissions

## 5. Typical Workflow Example
1. **Admin** creates and activates an appraisal cycle, then initiates appraisals
2. **Employees** fill out self-appraisals and update KPIs
3. **Line Managers** review appraisals, approve leave, and review KPIs
4. **Admin** monitors progress, manages assets, and views reports
